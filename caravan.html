NAVIGATION
This is the NAV tab. It displays information about what planet you are on, and which are available to travel to from here. You can also see things like local news, current sales on nearby planets, or pirate warnings.
CARGO
This is the CARGO tab. It displays information about what cargo you have in your ship, what goods are available to purchase, and prices at your current location. It also display how much fuel and ammo you have.
SHIP
This is the SHIP tab. It displays information about your ship, such as your engines and weapons. It is also where you can purchase upgrades for your cargo, comm array, weapons, defenses, engines, etc.
